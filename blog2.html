6 REASONS WHY DROP SHIPPING IS THE FUTURE OF ECOMMERCE

Over the decades we all know that eCommerce around the world has reached heights. People started to buy each and every product in eCommerce from apparel to electronics. Nowadays no one wants to go to the store, not for any item. According to online data portal sites, retail e-commerce deals worldwide numbered 2.3 trillion US dollars and are forecasted to grow more in the Future. And it is noticeable that there is a lot more t-shirt dropshipping in India compared to last year.

Dropshipping:

Dropshipping is a retailing industry model where a shop proprietor doesn’t store the product he markets in stock. As an outcome, the seller takes customer needs but does not maintain stock. He can contact his dropshipping dealer to supply the product on time to ship instantly to the customer.

And the most fascinating process is that the whole process is automatic. So here are some reasons why Dropshipping is the Future of eCommerce.

Product sourcing:

Established e-commerce shops must root production straight from the wholesalers, from different countries. The fundamental approach demands a ton of time, money, and aid. In addition, it frequently concerns the involvement of costly intermediaries, such as banks, cargo companies, and export importers.

The dropshipping model enables retailers to sell products without worrying about resourcing any item. In order to notify the wholesalers that their products will be brought by the retailer, the retailer can send an email.

Today, there is no better time than now to launch an online store because all logistics, warehouse, order picking, and packaging agreements, as well as other fulfillment services, can be handled by these companies.
