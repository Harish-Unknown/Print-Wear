The continued use of the Printwear.in site after any changes or amendments to this Privacy Policy shall signify the User’s acceptance of and consent to the changed or amended Privacy Policy.

PRINTWEAR.IN’S PRIVACY PLEDGE

Printwear.in is committed to protecting the privacy and personal information of Users at all times. Printwear.in recognizes that this information belongs to the User, and will treat such information accordingly. Printwear.in will not disclose any Personally Identifiable Information or confidential information of any User to any third parties, except as outlined in this Privacy Policy or as may be required by law.

PRINTWEAR.IN USE OF PERSONAL AND NON-PERSONAL INFORMATION

Printwear.in collects two kinds of information from on-line visitors, personal and non-personal information. Visitors to the Printwear.in Website are not required to register, or provide any Personally Identifiable Information to access the Printwear.in site or review the products and services offered. Printwear.in collects non-personal information to track the total number of guests visiting the Printwear.in site and to help Printwear.in improve the site and the services available on the site. Such non-personal information includes the browser type and operating system being used by visitors, areas of the sites visited, date and time of access, host or service provider information and identification of the referring site. This information is used exclusively for Printwear.in’s internal purposes and is not disclosed to any third parties. Personally Identifiable Information, which means any information that can be used to identify or locate a specific person, including, but not limited to e-mail addresses, names, street addresses, phone numbers, fax numbers, credit card numbers and social security numbers, is collected only when voluntarily offered and solely for purposes clearly identified on the Printwear.in site. Such Personally Identifiable Information is kept strictly confidential and will only be disclosed to third parties under the conditions described in Paragraph 5 of this Privacy Policy.

USE OF “COOKIES”

Printwear.in may place an electronic “Cookie” in the browser files of a visitor’s computer. Cookies are pieces of information that a web site transfers to an individual computer drive for record-keeping purposes. Cookies themselves do not enable Printwear.in to access any personal information about visitors. Cookies only allow Printwear.in to analyze a visitor’s use of the Printwear.in site. This analysis helps Printwear.in better understand which areas of the Printwear.in site are more useful and popular so that improvements and updates can be made in the future. Printwear.in does not use Cookies to retrieve information from a visitor’s computer that was not originally sent in a Cookie.
